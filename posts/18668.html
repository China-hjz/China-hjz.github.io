C - Sum of Product

题目分析

给长度 NNN 的数组 AAA ，求 ∑1≤i<j≤NAiAj\displaystyle \sum_{1\leq i< j\leq N}A_iA_j1≤i<j≤N∑​Ai​Aj​ 。

解题思路

先从样例一入手

表示为

∑1≤i<j≤NAiAj=A1A2+A1A3+A2A3\displaystyle \sum_{1\leq i< j\leq N} A_iA_j=A_1A_2+A_1A_3+A_2A_3 1≤i<j≤N∑​Ai​Aj​=A1​A2​+A1​A3​+A2​A3​

根据乘法结合律

∑1≤i<j≤NAiAj=A1A2+A1A3+A2A3=A1(A2+A3)+A2A3\displaystyle \sum_{1\leq i< j\leq N} A_iA_j=A_1A_2+A_1A_3+A_2A_3= A_1(A_2+A_3)+A_2A_3 1≤i<j≤N∑​Ai​Aj​=A1​A2​+A1​A3​+A2​A3​=A1​(A2​+A3​)+A2​A3​

发现

∑1≤i<j≤NAiAj=∑1≤i≤N(Ai(∑i+1≤j≤NAj)) \displaystyle \sum_{1\leq i< j\leq N} A_iA_j=\sum_{1\leq i\leq N}(A_i(\sum_{i+1\leq j\leq N}A_j)) 1≤i<j≤N∑​Ai​Aj​=1≤i≤N∑​(Ai​(i+1≤j≤N∑​Aj​))

用 前缀和 可以计算出 ∑i+1≤j≤NAj{\displaystyle \sum_{i+1\leq j\leq N}}A_ji+1≤j≤N∑​Aj​
